Method and formulas

Natural wet bulb is solved iteratively from dry bulb, dew point, and pressure using a Magnus saturation-vapor-pressure equation and a ventilated psychrometer relationship.

Outdoor direct-sun WBGT = 0.7 × natural wet bulb + 0.2 × globe temperature + 0.1 × dry bulb.

Shade/indoor WBGT = 0.7 × natural wet bulb + 0.3 × globe temperature.

Modeled globe temperature uses a simplified steady-state heat balance with solar gain, reflected solar adjustment, longwave radiation, and convective cooling. A measured globe temperature overrides the model.
